Quick Answers

Are classes live or recorded?
All our main courses are taught live, 1-on-1 via Zoom. We believe real-time interaction with a certified teacher is the most effective way to learn. We also provide recorded materials for revision.

Do you teach kids and adults?
Yes! We have specialized curriculums for children as young as 4 years old, as well as comprehensive programs for adults of all levels.

Can I pick my schedule?
Absolutely. Our teachers are available 24/7. You choose the days and times that perfectly fit your family's routine.

Do you have female teachers?
Yes, we have a large team of highly qualified female teachers available for female students and children.
