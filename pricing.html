FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do classes work?
Classes are held one-to-one via Zoom or Google Meet at your scheduled time. Each session is 30–60 minutes depending on your child's age and attention span. Parents can attend the first few sessions if needed.
Are teachers male and female?
Yes! We have both male and female teachers. You can request the gender you prefer for your child, and we will match you accordingly.
Can parents attend the classes?
Absolutely. Parents are welcome to sit in during classes, especially for younger children. Many parents find it helpful to stay involved in their child's learning journey.
What if I need to reschedule?
Life happens! You can reschedule or cancel sessions with 24 hours notice at no charge. We understand that family schedules change.
Can I switch packages later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your package at any time. We'll prorate the cost so you only pay for what you use.
Do you offer any discounts?
Yes! We offer sibling discounts (10% off for the second child), referral rewards, and occasional seasonal promotions. Contact us for more details.
