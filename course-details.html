Course Overview

Our Quran Memorization Program is designed to help students of all ages memorize the Quran with proper Tajweed and understanding. Whether you are starting from scratch or continuing your hifdh journey, our certified teachers will guide you every step of the way.

Each student receives a personalized memorization plan based on their current level, available time, and goals. We use a combination of new memorization, daily revision of previously memorized portions, and weekly assessments to ensure steady progress.
